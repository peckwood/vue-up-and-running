By default, it shows the lastname of author

We wish to show firstname, instead

We didn't follow textbook, but followed https://v2.vuejs.org/v2/guide/components-slots.html#Scoped-Slots

Note that the abbreviated syntax for default slot cannot be mixed with named slots, as it would lead to scope ambiguity:

Whenever there are multiple slots, use the full template based syntax for all slots:

{{slotProps.user.firstname}}
